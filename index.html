Creation Story
The beat was written on the toilet in just 10 minutes. It was originally planned as a super fast beat for Geometry Dash, but something entirely different came out. A snippet with the track collected 1.5M+ views on TikTok within two weeks after the beat dropped on streaming platforms. Later, artist «JayBZY!» hopped on the beat, and the simple instrumental got a second life as a full vocal version.

Who are you?
I’m Miralys (aka Miralys Scarlet). I’ve been 100% self-producing my shit since 2019. Everything you hear is made entirely on a smartphone. Zero PC projects. Just my thumbs and a screen.

Over the last 7 years, I’ve built a vault of 2,000+ demos, experimenting with everything from mainstream to the rawest underground. Back in the day, I dropped under different aliases and made meme tracks with my classmates—we had our school going crazy, but that vault stays locked (too local for y'all :)). I grew up on the golden era of internet memes (2000s - 2021). Most of the shit trending today is pure brainrot and degradation, so I decided to make my own wave.

I dropped my first official track as "Miralys" in October 2025, but the Miralys Scarlet project officially launched in January 2026 when I started taking socials seriously. Now? I make experimental music, break down my mobile projects on TikTok, post memes, and drop the lore behind the chaos.
How did you get into making music?
Shit just clicked, honestly. I was pressing random buttons for no reason. I only downloaded FL Mobile to play around with the piano, and ended up messing around till I started making beats. I have absolutely zero music theory background, no professional understanding of production, and zero clue about the physics of sound. If I mash random buttons and it sounds dope, the track is already a banger
